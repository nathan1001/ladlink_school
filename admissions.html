Welcome Message from the Head

By choosing LADLINK SCHOOL, you have chosen a school committed to exploring the best in everyone. Admissions
Admission Process
Completing the Application:

Hard copies of the completed application form are submitted to the Office. Download forms here.
The registration fee can ONLY BE PAID upon submission of the form to our Office; a cash payment in cedi equivalent of 70 cedis for the registration fee only for all applications (Nursery to Junior High)


NB: The School is unable to proceed with an application without all of step 1 being in place. The school also reserved the right to withhold tuition information until possibile admission of ward has been granted.


Entrance Assessment/Interview

English is the language of instruction, therefore, entrance exams are conducted in English.

All entry assessments (from Nursery to JHS 3) take place in June and July. Nursery children are tempted from any of these processed and parents are to discuss with the Principal.Kindergarteners go through a semi-formal interview with the Principal.

Lower and Upper Primary are assessed in Math and English; JHS One to Three are tested in Maths, English and Science.
The decision to make an offer of admissions is always approved by the Principal.

NB: If an application has been unsuccessful, parents will be notified by email or phone call and are required to indicate their intent to remain on the waiting list for the next academic year.

Payment of Fees & Documents PickUp

Once the child has received acceptance into the school, the appropriate admission and tuition fee payment must be paid before the start of the academic term. Late or midyear entrants will be given individual timeframes. When payment is made at any one of our designated banks (please see fee schedule), the deposit slip must be submitted to the Accounts Office as proof of payment. Cash or cheque payments are also accepted.

When the deposit slip from the bank is presented, an official receipt will be issued from the school. Parents are informed of all requested mandatory documentation including medical reports and previous school reports, and given a deadline for submission.

A ‘Welcome to LADLINK’ pack is given by the Office of Admissions

NB: Please note that all monetary transactions must be done through the Cashier, located on the ground floor of the Administration Block.

Orientation and School related information:

The Office of Admissions provides orientation events for all new families which are repeated over the academic year. An Information Pack is provided on uniforms and the daily requirements and procedures in the appropriate section(s) of the School.
